✦ About Us

Where Technology Meets Creativity

WandForge is a game & software startup founded by a software engineer and a game designer with a shared vision: building meaningful digital experiences through technology, creativity, and interactive storytelling.

WandForge was created with a simple belief: digital experiences should feel both technically polished and emotionally memorable.

By combining engineering, game design, and creative direction, we aim to build products that blend cozy fantasy aesthetics with scalable technology and thoughtful interaction design.

Our current focus is developing our debut cozy fantasy puzzle experience for mobile platforms while laying the foundation for a broader creative technology startup in the future.

We see WandForge not only as a game studio, but as a long-term creative venture where software, storytelling, and interactive design evolve together.
