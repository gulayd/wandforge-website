✦ About Us

Where Technology Meets Imagination

WandForge was founded by a software engineer and a game designer with a shared vision: creating meaningful digital experiences that blend creativity, gameplay, and technology.

WandForge is a creative game studio with a bold vision: to craft magical, meaningful, and player-centered games that inspire curiosity, creativity, and connection.

Our mission goes beyond entertainment. Through cozy fantasy games and educational game-based experiences, we aim to make play more accessible, learning more engaging, and digital experiences more emotionally meaningful.

Built by women striving to shape their place in the game industry, WandForge also aims to become a supportive creative hub for girls and young women entering the field a space where imagination, learning, and community grow together.
