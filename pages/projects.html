Dragon's Inn

A Cozy Fantasy Puzzle & Tavern Management Game

Dragon’s Inn is WandForge’s debut mobile game: a cozy fantasy puzzle and tavern management experience where players solve magical levels, collect rewards, and grow their own enchanted tavern.

Designed for players who enjoy relaxing gameplay, warm fantasy atmospheres, and satisfying progression, Dragon’s Inn blends puzzle-based challenges with tavern upgrades, stylized visuals, immersive UI/UX, and long term live product potential.

At WandForge, we combine technical execution with creative worldbuilding to craft scalable, player-centered game experiences that feel magical, meaningful, and memorable.
